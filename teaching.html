Teaching Resources

During the summer of 2022 I was lucky enough to teach as the instructor of record for Information Ethics and Tech Policy (developed and usually taught by Casey Fiesler). I loved teaching this course and enjoyed the challenge of distilling a whole semesters' worth of Casey's awesome materials into a short 5-week summer course. Below, I've included the materials that I developed for the class: slides, assignments, in-class discussion prompts, and final project instructions. All materials draw upon Casey's original assignments but have been significantly adjusted to create a curriculum that is suitable for a shorter summer semester. You can click into each week below to find the corresponding assignment and discussion documents. Final project instructions and the slide deck for the course are directly linked here.

The course is structured as such:
  • Week 1: Ethical Foundations
  • Week 2: Privacy and Research Ethics
  • Week 3: Speech and Content Policy
  • Week 4: Bias and Fairness
  • Week 5: Possibilities and Limitations of AI
