Fellowship Materials

Applying for fellowships is often stressed as important during your time in a PhD. However, I found that a lack of example materials made it difficult to know what made a successful (or unsuccessful) application. I've only applied to two fellowships, one that I won and another I was rejected from. Here I include my application materials for both fellowships. As I continue to apply to fellowships, I'll keep updating this page!
