About Me

I am a PhD student researching human-machine interaction and data governance in the context of the workplace. I am advised by Brian Keegan in the Column Lab.

Broadly, I am interested in theories of quantification, collective governance, and sensemaking. My research lies at the intersection of employment law, data governance, and systems design. I focus primarily on the data ecosystem that independent contractors operate in and contribute to. In particular, I explore the real-world implications of platform labor environments for both white and blue collar workers.

You can read more about my research projects here and my teaching experience here.
