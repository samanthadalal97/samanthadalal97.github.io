Reading List

Taking inspiration from Morgan Scheuerman's preliminary reading repository, I've decided to make one as well. Each entry contains a summary about the reading, key quotes, and a reflection on how it connects to my research trajectory. I hope that this abbreviated version of papers will be useful to future students (in addition to my future self).

You can filter the readings by type or theme by using the buttons below. Readings are in alphabetical order by author last name. Citation styles may vary.
